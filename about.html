🐾About Whiskers and Tails

Whiskers and Tails is a peaceful cat café located in the heart of chennai created for cat lovers and coffee enthusiasts. Our café offers a calm environment where visitors can relax while spending time with rescued cats.

All our cats are rescued and cared for with love. We follow strict hygiene standards and promote adoption awareness through our café.
